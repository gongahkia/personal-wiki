Gabriel Ong is a second-year undergraduate student reading Computing and Law at Singapore Management University. Gabriel works at the Centre for Digital Law based out of Singapore Management University, which researches legal technology through computer science. Here, Gabriel works primarily on developing experimental AI agents for use in legal theory. He has also copywritten and produced its documentation. In his free time, Gabriel enjoys writing software that benefits programmers, practising lawyers and law students. His latest efforts are in developing a cross-platform app for creatives. Gabriel is interested in pursuing the entrepreneurship of legal technology in Singapore later in his career.
